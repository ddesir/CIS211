A very dark text is also called a Bold text. For this purpose we use the Bold tag.

To emphasize the meaning of a word, we use the Underline tag.

A special effect is created by Blinking tag. It works in Netscape only.

To differentiate a text (word) from others, the Italic helps a lot.

The special style called Typewritten is similar to the one produced by a typing manual machine.

The Struck out font is mostly used to show corrections in a text.

A text moving horizontally from right to left, looks like a Slide dynamically changing different positions on the screen.
