The units of storage

It is highly important in technology to understand the
different units used to measure the capacity of devices

The smallest one is the bit

8 bits are equivalent to 1 Byte

1 Megabyte is equal to 1 million bytes

In scientific notation this may be expressed as

1 MB = 106 Bytes which is the same as

103 x 103 = 1000 x 1000 = 1 000 000
