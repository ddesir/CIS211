5 Emerging Technologies

  • Artificial Intelligence – Basically simulated intelligence in machines, where computers and other devices can make decisions and interact within certain parameters.
  • Machine Learning / Deep Learning – A method of analysis that automates analytical model building. It is a branch of artificial intelligence based on the idea that systems can learn from data, identify patterns and make decisions. Deep learning is the part of machine learning based on learning data representations, as opposed to task-specific algorithms.
  • Big Data – extremely large data sets that may be analyzed computationally to reveal patterns, trends, and associations, especially relating to human behavior and interactions.
  • Virtual Reality (VR) – visually and audibly immerses you in a virtual environment.
  • Augmented Reality – AR overlays elements over the physical world using a smartphone via its camera or in the case of Google Glass, enhanced eyewear.
