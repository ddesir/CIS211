Units of Processor Speed

Processor clock speed is measured in cycles per seconds or Hertz (Hz). Computer boards and CPUs run at rates of millions and billions of Hz, or megahertz (MHz) and gigahertz (GHz).

The machine I coded this page on runs at 2.9 GHz.
