Holidays

I have never been a fan of the holidays, but it's that time of the year in the city. Working in such a tourist heavy area has turned me off to the season even more. But there scenes that pop up are pretty nice.

Soon the tree at Rockefeller Center will be lit, the Radio City Rockettes will be on the street greeting people, the giant Nutcracker soldiers will be standing at attention.
