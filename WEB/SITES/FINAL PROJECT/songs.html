Songs I'm Listening To

I'm currently listening to the following song and I am moved by them, in no particular order:

  1. Good Food by Br3nya – Her first single. She's bragging about her life, how she looks, her dark skin, her assets, and her power over men. She has skill, mad sex appeal...
  2. Why DON'T You LOVE me? by Tory Lanes – Damn, I wished you loved me... And I can't make you love me... Why don't you love me? May be questions many of us have asked once or twice in our lives. It resonates with me.
  3. Found a Way by Dave East – It was so hard to get what I wanted, I found a way. I had to sacrifice for this life, I found a way. This has become my anthem. It's a reminder that I did what I had to do to get where I am and that I will continue to do so to get to where I'm going.
